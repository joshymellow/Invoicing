# 📑 Professional Work Log & Invoice Manager (v1.9)

A lightweight, browser-based invoicing tool designed for freelancers and independent contractors. Track your daily tasks, monitor your earnings in real-time, and generate professional, print-ready invoices in seconds.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🚀 Management Dashboard
* [cite_start]**Live Stats:** Real-time tracking of Total Hours, Average Daily Pay, and Active Task counts[cite: 1, 2].
* [cite_start]**Dynamic Meta Data:** Custom fields for your branding, client info, and specific invoice numbering[cite: 1, 2].

### 📝 Smart Task Logging
* [cite_start]**Grouped Entries:** Tasks are automatically sorted and grouped by date with automated sub-totals for each day[cite: 1, 2].
* [cite_start]**Bullet Point Formatting:** Supports multi-line comments that convert into professional bullet points on the final invoice[cite: 1, 2].
* [cite_start]**Quick Edit/Delete:** Pencil and trash icons allow for instant modification or removal of entries[cite: 2].

### 📜 History & Archiving
* [cite_start]**Close Month:** Archive your current logs to clear your workspace for a new month while keeping your data safe[cite: 1, 2].
* [cite_start]**History UI:** A dedicated modal to browse, restore, or delete past invoices[cite: 2].
* [cite_start]**Restore Logic:** Easily bring a past month back to the "live" view to make edits or re-print[cite: 2].

### 💾 Data Safety & Portability
* [cite_start]**JSON Backups:** Export your entire database to a local file for extra security[cite: 1, 2].
* [cite_start]**Easy Restore:** Import your backup files to sync data across different devices or browsers[cite: 1, 2].
* [cite_start]**Global Undo:** Safety nets for accidental "Clear All" or individual deletions[cite: 2].

### 🖨️ Professional Printing
* [cite_start]**Print-Ready Styles:** Built-in CSS hides all management buttons and forms when printing to PDF or paper[cite: 1].
* [cite_start]**Legal Size Support:** Optimized for 8.5" x 14" (Legal) layouts to accommodate long-form work logs[cite: 1].

## 🛠️ Installation & Setup

1. **Download the Files:** Clone or download `index.html`, `style.css`, and `script.js` into a single folder.
2. **Launch:** Open `index.html` in any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Edge recommended).
3. **Customize:** Enter your Name, Contact Info, and Hourly Rate in the top fields. [cite_start]These will be saved automatically for future use[cite: 2].

---

## 💡 How to Use

1. [cite_start]**Logging Work:** Select a date, enter your task description and duration, then click **"Add to Invoice"**[cite: 1].
2. [cite_start]**Finalizing:** Once the month is complete, use the **"Print / PDF"** button to generate your document[cite: 1].
3. [cite_start]**Archiving:** Click **"Close Month"** to move the completed tasks into your **History** and start a fresh log[cite: 2].
4. [cite_start]**Backing Up:** Regularly use **"Backup JSON"** to save a copy of your data to your hard drive[cite: 2].
