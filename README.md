# 📑 Professional Work Log & Invoice Manager (v1.8)

A lightweight, browser-based invoicing tool designed for freelancers and independent contractors. Track your daily tasks, monitor your earnings in real-time, and generate professional, print-ready invoices in seconds.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🚀 Management Dashboard
* **Live Stats:** Real-time tracking of Total Hours, Average Daily Pay, and Active Task counts.
* **Dynamic Meta Data:** Custom fields for your branding, client info, and specific invoice numbering.

### 📝 Smart Task Logging
* **Grouped Entries:** Tasks are automatically sorted and grouped by date with automated sub-totals for each day.
* **Bullet Point Formatting:** Supports multi-line comments that convert into professional bullet points on the final invoice.
* **Quick Edit/Delete:** Pencil and trash icons allow for instant modification or removal of entries.

### 📜 History & Archiving
* **Close Month:** Archive your current logs to clear your workspace for a new month while keeping your data safe.
* **History UI:** A dedicated modal to browse, restore, or delete past invoices.
* **Restore Logic:** Easily bring a past month back to the "live" view to make edits or re-print.

### 💾 Data Safety & Portability
* **JSON Backups:** Export your entire database to a local file for extra security.
* **Easy Restore:** Import your backup files to sync data across different devices or browsers.
* **Global Undo:** Safety nets for accidental "Clear All" or individual deletions.

---

## 🛠️ Installation & Setup

1. **Download the Files:** Clone or download `index.html`, `style.css`, and `script.js` into a single folder.
2. **Launch:** Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.
3. **Customize:** Enter your Name, Contact Info, and Hourly Rate in the top fields. These will be saved automatically for future use.

---

## 🎨 Themes
Use the **Theme Selector** in the header to switch between:
